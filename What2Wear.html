What Clothes to Wear on the Slopes

Base layers

Thermal underwear or moisture-wicking long-sleeve tops and leggings.

Insulating layers

Fleece or wool sweaters, vests, or jackets for added warmth.

Outerwear

Waterproof and breathable ski jacket and pants to protect against snow and wind.

Accessories

Insulated gloves or mittens, ski-specific socks, neck gaiter or scarf, beanie or hat, goggles or sunglasses with UV protection.

Extras

Hand warmers, ear warmers or earmuffs, sunscreen for exposed skin, lip balm with SPF, ski-specific socks, and a helmet for safety.
